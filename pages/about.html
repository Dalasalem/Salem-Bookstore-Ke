Our Story

Salem Bookstore KE was founded in 2017 with a simple vision: to make Christian resources accessible and affordable to every believer in Kenya. What started as a small shop in Migori has grown into a trusted destination for churches, individuals, and ministries across the country and all over the world.

Our name "Salem" means peace, and we believe that true peace comes from knowing God through His Word. Every book we sell, every Bible we distribute, is part of our commitment to spreading this peace throughout Kenya

Our founder believed that access to God's Word and quality Christian literature should not be limited by economics or geography. Over the years, we have expanded our inventory, improved our services, and built meaningful partnerships with churches and ministry organizations.

Today, Salem Bookstore KE is proud to serve thousands of customers annually, providing not just products but also guidance, support, and community. We have been instrumental in supporting literacy programs, church outreach initiatives, and spiritual development projects across Kenya.

Our commitment remains unchanged: to nurture faith, inspire growth, and be a beacon of Christian resources for every believer. Whether you're seeking a Bible for personal study, resources for your Sunday school, or materials for a ministry project, Salem Bookstore KE is here to serve you.

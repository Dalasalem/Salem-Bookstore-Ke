Tips for Your Visit

🕐 Best Times to Visit

We're most relaxed during weekday mornings (9-11 AM) and early afternoons (2-4 PM). Feel free to browse unhurried during these times. Saturdays are busier but still welcoming!

📋 For Bulk Orders

If you're planning to purchase large quantities for your church, school, or ministry, we recommend calling ahead at +254 712 345 678. We can prepare everything and potentially arrange bulk pricing.

🚗 Parking & Accessibility

Our Migori store has nearby parking on Manyera CBD. The store is wheelchair accessible and has facilities for comfortable browsing. If you have special accessibility needs, please let us know in advance.

🎯 Special Requests

Looking for a specific Bible translation or hard-to-find book? Contact us before visiting and we'll try to source it for you. We can often get special orders within 1-2 weeks.

📱 Stay Connected

Follow our social media for updates on new arrivals, special promotions, and upcoming events. Join our WhatsApp community for exclusive deals and prayer requests.

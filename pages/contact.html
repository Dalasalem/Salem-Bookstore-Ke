Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery throughout Kenya. Delivery costs depend on location and order size. Contact us or add items to cart for delivery options.

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, cash, and bank transfers. For bulk orders, we can arrange special payment terms.

❓ Do you have discounts for bulk orders?

Yes! Churches, schools, and ministries qualify for significant bulk discounts. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

❓ Can I special order specific Bibles or books?

Absolutely! If we don't have a specific title or Bible translation in stock, we can usually source it for you within 1-2 weeks. Contact us with your request.

❓ Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! We offer beautiful gift wrapping for most items. Perfect for showing your care when giving meaningful Christian gifts. A small gift-wrapping fee applies.

❓ Are products guaranteed to be authentic?

Yes! Every product sold at Salem Bookstore KE is authentic and quality-checked. We work only with authorized distributors and reputable publishers.

❓ Do you have a return policy?

We stand behind all products. If you receive an item with defects or damage, contact us immediately for return or replacement. Details available in-store or on request.

❓ Can your team help me choose a Bible?

Absolutely! Our staff is knowledgeable about Bible translations and can help based on your needs—whether for study, devotion, family reading, or a specific age group.

❓ Do you support churches and ministries?

Yes! We have specialized programs for churches, schools, and ministries including bulk pricing, custom sourcing, and flexible payment terms. Contact our ministry team.

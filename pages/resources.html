Our Journey — 2017 to 2026

A brief timeline of milestones and moments that shaped Salem Bookstore KE.

2017

Founded

Salem Bookstore opened its doors in Migori, focused on providing trusted Christian resources and Bibles to our community.

2018

Community Outreach

Started outreach programs and mobile stalls to bring Scripture and reading materials to local events and schools.

2019

Online Presence

Launched our online pages and began sharing articles, reading plans, and devotional materials digitally.

2020

Support During Hard Times

Adapted to the needs of our community by providing remote study resources and free downloadable materials during the pandemic.

2021

Growth in Programs

Expanded Bible study guides and launched local small-group initiatives to strengthen discipleship and fellowship.

2022

Partnerships & Expansion

Partnered with local ministries and broadened our inventory to include more study tools and faith-based literature.

2023

Ministry Collaborations

Worked closely with churches and ministries to provide materials for outreach and discipleship programs.

2024

Digital Resources

Introduced more downloadable study guides and tools to help believers study God's Word anywhere.

2025

Wider Reach

Reached readers and study groups across Kenya with curated content, events, and online resources.

2026

Continuing the Mission

Celebrating nine years of serving Kenya with trusted Christian resources and continuing to support spiritual growth.
